Customer Support Compliance Policy

Refund Policy:
Customers are eligible for refunds within 7 calendar days from the purchase date if the product has not been misused. A valid invoice number and customer verification are required before processing any refund request. Unauthorized refunds are considered a compliance violation.

Communication Guidelines:
Support agents must use professional language, greet the customer properly, verify identity, and follow the approved response script. Any rude language, missing greeting, or incomplete response must be marked as a quality issue.

Escalation Policy:
If the issue cannot be resolved within the first interaction, the case must be escalated to Level 2 support. Billing, payment, and security related issues must be escalated immediately.

Data Privacy Rules:
Agents must never share passwords, OTP, credit card details, or personal information in chat or call transcripts. Any such action must be flagged as a high severity violation.

Call Handling Standard:
Every support interaction must include greeting, issue identification, resolution attempt, confirmation, and closing statement. Missing steps should reduce quality score.
